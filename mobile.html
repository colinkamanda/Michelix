What is a Mobile Application ?

A mobile App (or mobile application) is a software application developed speciffically for use on small, wireless computing devices, such as Smartphones and tablets, rather than desktop or laptop computers.
Mobile apps are sometimes categorized according to the whether they are web-based or given platform. A third category, hybrid apps, combines elements of both native and web apps. In today's digital age, mobile apps are an essantial part of most people's daily lives.
From social networking and entertainement to productivity and bussinesses, mobile apps play a vital role in how we interact with Technology.
